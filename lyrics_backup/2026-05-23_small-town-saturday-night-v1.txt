[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Pour the whiskey slow, let the shadows fall
Just you and me beneath this neon on the wall
Got the whole world waiting, but I'm staying right here
Losing track of time whenever you are near

[Verse 1]
The gravel roads are quiet, the moonlight is our guide
I pulled my truck over just to have you by my side
You give me that look, the one that starts a fire
Every breath you take is feeding my desire

[Pre-Chorus]
Lean a little closer, let the engine cool down
We are the only beating hearts left in this sleepy town
Your hand in mine, feeling the spark ignite
I could hold you like this for the rest of my life

[Chorus]
Oh, it is a small town Saturday night with you
There is nothing else on earth that I would rather do
Tracing out the stars shining in your eyes
Getting lost together in this sweet midnight

(Lost with you, baby)
